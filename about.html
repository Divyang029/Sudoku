About Sudoku

What is Sudoku?

Sudoku is a popular logic-based number puzzle. The objective of the game is to fill a 9x9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3x3 sub-grids (regions) contains all the numbers from 1 to 9, with no repetition.

History of Sudoku

Sudoku puzzles originated in Switzerland in the late 18th century and became widely known in Japan in the 1980s. They have since gained global popularity and have been featured in newspapers, magazines, and online puzzle games.
